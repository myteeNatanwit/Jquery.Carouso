idea

It is a carousel slide show but with the pic searched from GG "girl model png". They are all Alpha png. I run the free application "image tuner" to batch resize them to 320x480. I put in the background pic from deviantart. renamed it to bg.jpg. You can replace it with anything u want anyway.

Usage

There r 9 pics of girl. you just change them in index.html to anything u like. The Css3 file is with the ribbon effects for H1 and H2. Note that h2 has 2 classed left and right. Each odd pic goes with h2.left and so on with the even pic with h2.right. The names in there r just those came to my mind at that time. Not in any particular order or selection. They are all fictional.
